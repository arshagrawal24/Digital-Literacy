# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Always verify the sender before clicking on any link or opening attachments
- Do not share personal information like passwords or OTPs with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for extra security
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
- Use only trusted websites and check for "https://" in the URL

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Always double-check UPI ID or phone number before sending money
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept random payment requests
- Never approve payment requests from unknown persons

## Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930 (24x7)
