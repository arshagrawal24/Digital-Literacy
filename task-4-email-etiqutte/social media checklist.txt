Part B: Social Media Do’s & Don’ts

✅ Do’s
1.Maintain a professional profile
2.Share useful and positive content
3.Use privacy settings properly
4.Respect others’ opinions
5.Think before posting anything

❌ Don’ts
1.Do not share personal/confidential information
2.Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
3.Do not engage in online arguments
4.Avoid spreading fake news
5.Do not use abusive or disrespectful language
